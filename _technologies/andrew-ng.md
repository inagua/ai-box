Andrew Ng is a prominent computer scientist, educator, and entrepreneur in the field of artificial intelligence. He is a co-founder of Coursera and Google Brain, and a former Chief Scientist at Baidu. He is currently an Adjunct Professor at Stanford University and the founder of DeepLearning.AI and Landing AI. He is widely recognized for his contributions to deep learning and his efforts to make AI education accessible to millions.
