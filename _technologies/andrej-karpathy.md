Andrej Karpathy is a prominent AI researcher and a founding member of [OpenAI]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/openai/). He previously served as the Director of AI at Tesla, where he led the computer vision team for Autopilot. He is also well-known for his educational work in deep learning, particularly his "Zero to Hero" series and his influential blog.
