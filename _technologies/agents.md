AI Agents are autonomous entities that perceive their environment and take actions to achieve goals. They are the core components of [Agentic]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/agentic/) systems, enabling automated workflows and complex problem-solving. When multiple agents work together, it is often referred to as an [Agent Swarm]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/agent-swarm/).
