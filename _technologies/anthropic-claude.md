Claude is a next-generation AI assistant based on [Anthropic]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/anthropic/)’s research into training helpful, honest, and harmless AI systems. Accessible via chat interface and API, Claude is capable of a wide variety of conversational and text processing tasks while maintaining a high degree of reliability and predictability.

Claude is known for its large context windows and its focus on "Constitutional AI," which uses a set of principles to guide the AI's behavior and safety.
