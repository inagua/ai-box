Aravind Srinivas is the co-founder and CEO of **Perplexity AI**, an AI-native search engine that provides direct answers to queries using large language models. Before founding Perplexity, he was a researcher at OpenAI and Google DeepMind, specializing in reinforcement learning and generative models.
