Llama (Large Language Model Meta AI) is a family of autoregressive large language models, released by Meta AI. Llama 2 and its successors are open-weight models, allowing researchers and developers to build upon them freely for a wide range of applications.

Meta’s Llama models have been highly influential in the open-source AI community, enabling broad experimentation and deployment of capable AI systems.
