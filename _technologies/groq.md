Groq is an AI infrastructure company that has developed a specialized processor called the LPU™ (Language Processing Unit™). It is designed specifically for large language models, offering significantly faster inference speeds than traditional GPUs for text generation tasks. Groq's architecture focuses on deterministic performance and high throughput, making it ideal for real-time AI applications.
