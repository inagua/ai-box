GPT-4 is a large multimodal model (accepting image and text inputs, emitting text outputs) that, while less capable than humans in many real-world scenarios, exhibits human-level performance on various professional and academic benchmarks.

It is part of [OpenAI]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/openai/)’s GPT series, which has revolutionized the AI landscape by providing highly capable conversational and reasoning capabilities that can be integrated into various applications through APIs.
