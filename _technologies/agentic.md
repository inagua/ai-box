Agentic AI refers to systems designed to exhibit agency—the ability to take independent action to achieve specific goals. Unlike traditional AI that simply predicts or classifies, agentic systems use [AI Agents]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/agents/) to interact with their environment and perform multi-step tasks. One prominent pattern for agentic systems is the [Agent Swarm]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/agent-swarm/).
