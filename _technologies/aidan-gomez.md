Aidan Gomez is the co-founder and CEO of Cohere, a leading AI company specializing in Large Language Models for the enterprise. He is notably a co-author of the seminal paper "Attention Is All You Need," which introduced the [Transformer]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/transformer/) architecture that underpins almost all modern AI. Before Cohere, he was a researcher at Google Brain.
