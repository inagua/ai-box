Google DeepMind is Google's primary AI research division led by [Demis Hassabis]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/demis-hassabis/), formed by merging the DeepMind and Google Brain teams. They are responsible for Gemini, AlphaGo, and many other breakthroughs in machine learning and artificial intelligence.
