JetBrains Air is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance the development and collaboration experience within the JetBrains ecosystem. It leverages advanced language models to provide context-aware assistance, automate repetitive tasks, and improve overall productivity for developers and teams.

The platform is closely integrated with JetBrains' suite of IDEs and tools, offering features such as intelligent code completion, automated documentation generation, and real-time support for various programming languages and frameworks.
