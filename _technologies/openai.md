OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company led by [Sam Altman]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/sam-altman/). Their mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. They are known for developing [GPT series models]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/openai-gpt4/), DALL-E, and Sora.
