**Grok-4** is the fourth generation of large language models developed by **xAI**. It is designed to be a highly efficient, multi-modal model with real-time access to the X platform (formerly Twitter) for up-to-the-minute information. It continues the trend of models with fewer filters and a more "direct" personality.
