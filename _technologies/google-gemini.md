Gemini is Google's largest and most capable AI model. It was built from the ground up to be multimodal, which means it can generalize and seamlessly understand, operate across and combine different types of information including text, code, audio, image and video.

Gemini comes in different sizes (Pro, Ultra, Flash) to suit different tasks, ranging from complex reasoning to efficient real-time interactions.
