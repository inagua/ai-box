Elon Musk is the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, and the founder of **xAI**. He was also one of the original co-founders of [OpenAI]({{ site.baseurl }}/technologies/openai/). Through xAI, he has overseen the development of the Grok series of Large Language Models, which are integrated into the X (formerly Twitter) platform. He is a prominent figure in the AI community, often discussing the existential risks and potential benefits of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI).
