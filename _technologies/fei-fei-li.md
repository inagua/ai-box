Dr. Fei-Fei Li is the Sequoia Professor of Computer Science at Stanford University and the Co-Director of the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered AI (HAI). She is widely known for her pioneering work in computer vision, particularly the creation of ImageNet, which was instrumental in the deep learning revolution. She is often called the "Godmother of AI" for her contributions to the field and her advocacy for human-centered technology.
