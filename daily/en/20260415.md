## 📈 AI Trend
**World Models: AI That Understands the Physics of the World**

"World Models" are becoming the new frontier of generative AI. Unlike traditional LLMs that predict the next word, these models learn to simulate physical laws and the consequences of actions in a given environment, whether virtual or real.

This technology is crucial for the development of autonomous robotics and driverless vehicles. By understanding how objects interact with each other, AI can plan trajectories and anticipate collisions with much greater precision than purely statistical methods.

Eventually, world models will allow for the creation of ultra-realistic simulations for training other AIs, thereby accelerating reinforcement learning without the risk of physical damage. This is a giant leap toward an AI capable of interacting naturally with our physical reality.

- *Status: Volatile*

## 💡 Key Concept: Direct Preference Optimization (DPO)
Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) is an innovative method for aligning language models with human preferences. Unlike traditional RLHF, DPO does not require training a separate reward model, making the alignment process more stable, simpler, and more efficient.
