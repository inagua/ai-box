## 📈 AI Trend
**Agentic reasoning over model size**
The latest trend in AI is moving away from simply building larger models and instead focusing on how models use their reasoning capabilities in iterative loops. This "agentic" approach allows smaller models to outperform much larger ones by reflecting on their work and self-correcting.

As the industry hits a "compute wall," efficiency becomes paramount. By breaking down tasks into sub-problems and using specialized tools, AI systems can solve complex engineering and research problems that were previously out of reach for a single forward pass of a massive LLM.

The evolution of this trend is leading to "Agent-as-a-Service" platforms where users don't just buy access to a model, but to an autonomous agent capable of fulfilling high-level goals with minimal supervision.
- *Status: Volatile*
