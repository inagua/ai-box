## 📈 AI Trend
**The Rise of AI-Driven Personalized Education**

AI is radically transforming the education sector by enabling learning personalization at a scale never seen before. Intelligent tutoring systems now analyze each student's strengths and weaknesses in real-time to adapt pedagogical content and learning pace.

## 💡 Key Concept: RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is an architecture that allows an LLM to access external data in real-time to improve the accuracy and freshness of its responses. This helps reduce hallucinations by grounding answers in verifiable facts.
