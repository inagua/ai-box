## 📈 AI Trend
**Trustworthy AI and Ethics by Design**

In 2026, the major trend is shifting from mere computing power to "Trustworthy AI." Companies are now prioritizing models whose decisions are explainable and whose biases are actively controlled from the training phase, rather than corrected after the fact.

This "Ethics by Design" approach integrates safeguards directly into the neural network architecture. This not only complies with increasingly strict international regulations but also ensures increased security in critical sectors such as healthcare or finance.

The challenge is no longer just knowing what AI can do, but how it does it responsibly. Real-time monitoring tools and independent audits are becoming the norm for any large-scale AI integration.

- *Status: Stable*

## 💡 Key Concept: Sparse Priming Representations (SPR)
Sparse Priming Representations (SPR) is an information compression technique allowing LLMs to process huge volumes of data extremely efficiently. By using dense semantic "primes," the necessary context can be loaded into the model without saturating its context window.
