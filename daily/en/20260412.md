## 📈 AI Trend
**Dynamic Multi-Agent Orchestration**

We are witnessing a transition from using single agents to multi-agent systems where roles are assigned dynamically based on the task at hand. Instead of having a rigid workflow, these systems can self-organize a team of specialized agents to solve unforeseen problems.

This trend is facilitated by new inter-agent communication protocols that allow for a more structured and efficient exchange of information. The evolution focuses on the system's ability to evaluate its own performance and adjust the composition of its agent "swarm" in real-time.

Ultimately, this will enable the creation of AI systems capable of managing entire projects, from design to delivery, with minimal human supervision. However, the complexity of these interactions requires more sophisticated debugging and monitoring tools.

- *Status: Volatile*
