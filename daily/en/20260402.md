## 📈 AI Trend
**Agentic Workflows**
The industry is moving from single-turn chat interactions to complex agentic workflows where AI models plan, use tools, and iterate to complete multi-step goals.
This shift emphasizes the importance of reasoning and self-correction, as models are increasingly tasked with executing real-world operations rather than just generating text.
As these systems evolve, the focus is transitioning from model size to the efficiency and reliability of the agent architecture itself.
- *Status: Volatile*
