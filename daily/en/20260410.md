## 📈 AI Trend
**Small Models for Local Real-Time Execution**

The trend of "Small Language Models" (SLMs) is accelerating as they become more efficient and capable. Organizations are increasingly deploying smaller models directly on edge devices to ensure data privacy, reduce latency, and lower operational costs.

This evolution is driven by advanced quantization techniques and distillation processes where larger models teach smaller ones. This allows for high-quality AI interaction on smartphones and laptops without a permanent internet connection.

As SLMs continue to improve, we are seeing a shift towards local-first AI architectures. This trend is expected to redefine the relationship between users and their data, making AI a more private and ubiquitous part of everyday technology.

- *Status: Volatile*

## 💡 Key Concept: Agentic RAG
Agentic RAG represents a shift from traditional retrieval-augmented generation to a more autonomous system. Instead of following a fixed search-and-generate flow, an AI agent decides what information is needed, searches dynamically, evaluates results, and iterates until it has a complete answer.
