## 📈 AI Trend
**World Models and Spatial Intelligence**
The focus of AI research is shifting toward "World Models," which aim to give AI systems a deeper understanding of physical reality and 3D space. This evolution is moving beyond text-based reasoning toward models that can predict and simulate the consequences of actions within a physical environment.

Spatial intelligence is becoming a cornerstone for the next generation of robotics and autonomous systems. By learning from video and physical data, these models are developing a "common sense" about the world that previous LLMs lacked.

This trend is expected to bridge the gap between digital assistants and embodied AI that can interact safely and effectively in the real world. Companies like World Labs are leading this charge, pushing the boundaries of what AI can perceive and do.
- *Status: Volatile*
