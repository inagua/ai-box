## 💡 Key Concept: Agentic AI
**Agentic AI** refers to systems designed to exhibit agency—the ability to take independent action to achieve specific goals. Unlike traditional AI that simply predicts or classifies, agentic systems use AI agents to interact with their environment and perform multi-step tasks autonomously. This concept is at the heart of the current evolution toward intelligent and complex automation.
